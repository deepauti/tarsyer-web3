CASE STUDY

A large Indian cement manufacturing company was struggling with managing inventory in their distribution network. With warehouses in remote locations and large volumes moving through each warehouse, there were several cost leakages that our customer was struggling to plug. A lack of visibility into the transhipments and diversions at each location led them to overpaying logistics costs.


By deploying our computer vision algorithms the customer was able to get complete visibility into operations. Automated dashboards gave them daily visibility into

   • Diversions and transhipments
   • The actual handling cost
   • The correct inventory in the warehouse
   • Operating hours of the warehouse
   • Typical loading and unloading times

About us

We are a group of computer vision engineers who started on our journey in 2020 and work out of one of the leafy bylanes of Koregaon Park, Pune. By staying away from the noisier and bustling areas of the city, we are able to devote all our energies to developing and optimizing and vision algorithms to solve ever more complex problems.

This is backed by many years spent in the trenches developing and deploying complex technology solutions to optimize manufacturing and warehousing processes.

While there are many great technologies out there, we decide to focus all our energies on computer vision. After all, 30% of our brain is dedicated to visual processing. So, if we can solve the vision problem, then we can solve a wide variety of problems in several industries.

Another driving force for us was to go deep into the business problem and not get bogged down by the plumbing of tech complexity. This is the genesis of the Tarsyer Edge Device.  All our algorithms, IP and software are packaged into this single device. For you, as the user, you need just need this device and can ignore everything else.

Our expertise lies in manufacturing, warehousing and security but we are always looking to solving complex problems. We are always on the lookout for more challenges, so if you have something in mind that we don’t yet provide, we’d love to hear about it.


The Name Game
The rationale behind Tarsyer..

Why do we call ourselves Tarsyer? The Tarsier is a tiny primate found in the jungles of SE Asia. Among land animals it has the largest eyes in relation to its body and Its acute sense of vision helps this this seemingly defenceless creature thrive in the brutal jungle environment.

You see the connection! Our excellent vision (algorithms) will help you (and us) thrive and surpass the competition in this tough business world.

A passing similarity to Master Yoda? Yes, of course that was intentional! We are the Jedi that will get rid of the wastage and leakages in all your operations and help you move from the dark side (inefficiency) to the light (high productivity)
